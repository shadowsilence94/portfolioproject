About Me

Hello! I'm Htut Ko Ko, a Data Science and Machine Learning enthusiast with a background in Marine Engineering. I hold a Bachelor's of Engineering in Marine Engineering from Myanmar Maritime University and have worked across diverse industries, from logistics and marine engineering to business development and data analysis.

My journey into data science began during my work as a Business Development Executive, where I recognized the power of data-driven decision-making. I used data analysis techniques to optimize sales action plans and implement ISO/HACCP certification processes. This experience sparked my interest in machine learning, data engineering, and artificial intelligence, and since then, I’ve been building my expertise in these areas. With a strong foundation in tools such as Python, R, SQL, and proficiency in visualization tools like Tableau and matplotlib, I focus on delivering actionable insights that drive business impact. Whether it's performing exploratory data analysis, building predictive models, or designing interactive dashboards, I enjoy helping organizations make data-informed decisions.

Currently, I'm expanding my skills in web development, integrating my data science expertise with technologies like HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap to create dynamic data-driven applications. As I continue to grow in this space, my ultimate goal is to build innovative solutions that bridge the gap between data and decision-making. I’m passionate about lifelong learning, and I'm actively pursuing courses in AI, machine learning, and advanced analytics. My diverse technical background and analytical mindset drive me to solve complex problems and develop efficient, scalable solutions for real-world challenges.
